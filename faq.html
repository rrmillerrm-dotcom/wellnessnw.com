The work

My approach is relational, neuroaffirming, and rooted in Internal Family Systems (IFS). I don't believe in "fixing" you because I don't believe you are broken.

Instead, we work together to understand the different parts of you — the parts that strive, the parts that protect, and the parts that hurt. By building a relationship with these internal parts, we can unburden the pain they carry and help you lead from a place of calm and confidence.

We check in regularly on your goals and progress to make sure our work together continues to feel worthwhile to you.

Getting started

Before your first session, you'll receive a link to my client portal. You'll be asked to complete basic demographic information, a brief history, and your treatment goals. There may also be brief symptom checklists, which help us track progress over time.

Sessions are typically scheduled on a weekly or biweekly recurring basis. Sessions run 45-55 minutes. Extended or more frequent sessions are available on a case-by-case basis when the work calls for it.

Privacy & payment

I am ethically and legally obligated to keep records of your treatment, which typically include a diagnosis, treatment plan, symptoms, interventions, safety concerns, and progress.

If you use a superbill for out-of-network reimbursement, the information shared with your insurance company includes your diagnosis, date of service, and session length. Insurance plans may also request progress notes or treatment summaries.

There are legal limits to confidentiality. I am required to break confidentiality in the following situations:

  • If I believe you are in imminent danger of harming yourself or someone else
  • If I suspect abuse or neglect of a child, elderly person, or vulnerable adult
  • If I am ordered by a court of law to release records

My fee is $220 per 45-55 minute session. I am an out-of-network provider and do not bill insurance directly.

I can provide a monthly superbill that you can submit to your insurance company for potential out-of-network reimbursement. I recommend calling your insurance provider to ask about your out-of-network mental health benefits before we begin.

Payment is due at the time of service. I accept HSA/FSA cards and all major credit cards. See the rates page for full details including the cancellation policy.
