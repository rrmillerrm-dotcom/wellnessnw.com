Resources

Support outside the therapy room.

A curated list of crisis lines, therapist directories, and additional resources. If you're not sure where to start, feel free to reach out.

Crisis support — available 24/7
If you're in crisis, please reach out now.
These lines are free, confidential, and available around the clock.
988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
Free and confidential support for people in distress, plus prevention and crisis resources.
Call or text 988
Crisis Text Line
Text with a trained Crisis Counselor from anywhere in the US, any time of day.
Text HOME to 741741
National DV Hotline
24/7 confidential support for people experiencing domestic violence.
Call 800-799-SAFE
